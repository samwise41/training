### Part 2: Ride Day Mixing Protocol
*When it's time to ride, grab one baggie from the pantry and your precision glass droppers.*

1.  **Heat the Water:** Heat exactly **140 grams (140 ml)** of water until it is steaming hot.
2.  **Dissolve:** Dump the entire contents of your dry baggie into the hot water. Whisk continuously until the powder dissolves and turns into a clear syrup.
3.  **Add Flavor:** Use your glass dropper to add your flavor drops (see menu below). 
    * *The Golden Rule:* If you are using **Extracts** (alcohol-based), add them *immediately* while the syrup is still steaming hot so the alcohol flashes off. If you are using **Emulsions** (water-based), you can add them at any point before it cools.
4.  **Cool & Bottle:** Let the syrup cool to room temperature, then pour it into your 8 oz soft flask. 

*(Note: If you are doing a massive ride and want to fill your big 16 oz flask, just heat 280g of water and dump in TWO baggies!)*
